Link Cleaner Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026

Information We Do Not Collect

Link Cleaner does not send the original links you check, your warning list, your allow list, or reward status to a developer server. The app does not use a developer account system or a developer-operated backend.

On-Device Processing

Link analysis, tracking-parameter removal, and user rule storage are processed on your device. The Safari extension checks links on webpages, but it does not show ads inside Safari.

Ads

The app may show Google AdMob ads to keep the app free. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may process device identifiers, advertising data, usage data, diagnostics, and related information for ad delivery, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and ad quality. The app may request iOS tracking permission for personalized ads. You can still use the core link-checking and link-cleaning features if you do not allow tracking.
