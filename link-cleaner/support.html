Link Cleaner Support

Turn On The Safari Extension

Open iPhone Settings > Safari > Extensions, then enable Link Cleaner.

Links From Other Apps

Link Cleaner cannot directly change screens inside apps such as Instagram, YouTube, Naver, or KakaoTalk. Use the share button and choose Link Cleaner to check a shared link.

Short Links

The serverless version does not automatically expand short links to their final destination. Short links are flagged before you open them.
