Link Cleaner

Link Cleaner helps you check tracking, shopping, and short links before they open in Safari. When removable tracking values are found, it can open a cleaner URL.

Features

  • Tap-before-open confirmation in Safari
  • Cleaner URL option for removable tracking values
  • Warnings for shopping links and short links
  • Personal warning and allow lists
  • Share extension for links from other apps

Ads

The app may show Google AdMob ads to keep the core app free. Ads appear only in the main app and are not shown inside the Safari extension.
