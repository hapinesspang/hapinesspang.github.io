Home Measurement Toolkit

A quick iPhone utility for checking level, angle, length, direction, and surrounding sound level.

Support Privacy Policy

Home Measurement Toolkit combines a level, angle meter, ruler, compass, sound meter, and saved measurement history in one iPhone app.

Measurements are for everyday reference only. Values may vary by device, case, environment, and sensor conditions, and are not a substitute for professional measuring equipment.

Main Features

  • Check tilt and angle with the level and angle meter
  • Use the on-screen ruler for quick reference length checks
  • View magnetic north direction with the compass
  • Reference surrounding sound level with the sound meter
  • Save and delete measurement records on your device

Privacy and Ads

The app does not require account creation or login. Saved measurement records remain on your device, and the app does not send measurement records to a developer-operated server.

The app may show Google AdMob ads to keep it free. It does not offer in-app purchases or subscriptions. Personalized ads are used only when permitted by the user, and all measurement tools, saved records, and settings remain available even if tracking is not allowed.
