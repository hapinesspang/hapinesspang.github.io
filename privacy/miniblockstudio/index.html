Mini Block Studio Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-21

Privacy details for guided builds, Free Studio creations, and the local shelf.

Operator: Yoobin Shin

Contact: hapinesspang@gmail.com

1. App Features

Mini Block Studio lets users build guided 3D mini block models, remix Free Studio starter ideas, create small custom builds, save local progress, keep completed models on a shelf, and share finished builds through the iOS share sheet.

2. Account and Server Use

The app does not require sign-in and does not create app operator accounts. Version 1.0 is serverless for app-created content. Guided progress, completed model IDs, custom builds, and badge progress are stored locally on the user's device.

3. Information Stored by the App

The app may store the following information locally on the device:

  • Completed guided model IDs
  • In-progress guided build piece counts
  • Custom build names and block layouts
  • Build completion counters used for badges

This local information is not uploaded to an app operator server in version 1.0.

4. Sharing

Sharing is user initiated. When a user taps a share button, iOS presents the system share sheet. The user chooses the destination, and the app does not control or store the data after it is shared to another app or service.

5. Advertising and Tracking

Version 1.0 is ad-free and does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

The app does not request device identifiers for tracking in version 1.0.

6. Third-Party Services

Version 1.0 does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Local progress and custom builds remain on the device unless the user deletes saved creations in the app, deletes the app, or removes related app data according to iOS behavior. If the app is deleted, app-local data may be deleted according to iOS behavior.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children and is not operated as a service for children under 13.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: hapinesspang@gmail.com
