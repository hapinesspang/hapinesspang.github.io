Photo Resize Compressor Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-16

Operator: Yoobin Shin

Contact: hapinesspang@gmail.com

1. App Features

Photo Resize Compressor lets users select photos, reduce file size, resize images, choose output formats, and save or share converted image files.

2. Photo Processing

The app processes only photos selected by the user. Original photos are not modified. Converted images are saved as new files. Image processing is performed on the device, and photos are not uploaded to the app operator's server.

3. Photo Library Access

The app uses the iOS photo picker to let users select photos. When saving converted images to the Photos app, the app may request add-only photo library permission.

4. Information Stored by the App

The current version does not store selected original photos or converted images on an app operator server. Basic settings, such as selected conversion options and ad display counters, may be stored on the device.

5. Advertising

The app may use Google AdMob ads to keep the app free. During ad delivery, the Google Mobile Ads SDK and related Google services may process information for ad serving, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and ad quality improvement.

Examples of information that may be processed include:

  • Approximate location inferred from IP address
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA), if the user allows tracking
  • Ad impressions and ad interaction information
  • Ad request and ad performance information
  • Product interaction and app activity information
  • Device and app information
  • Crash, diagnostic, and performance information

6. Personalized Ads and Tracking Permission

The app may request Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission for personalized ads and ad performance measurement. All image conversion features remain available if the user does not allow tracking.

If tracking is allowed, the advertising identifier may be used for personalized ads and ad measurement. If tracking is not allowed, ads may be non-personalized or limited.

The app disables the Google Mobile Ads SDK publisher first-party ID feature so that the app does not add that same-app key as an alternate identifier when tracking is denied.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK
  • Google User Messaging Platform SDK

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Converted images are saved only where the user chooses, such as the Photos app or a share destination. If the app is deleted, app-local settings may be deleted according to iOS behavior.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children and is not operated as a service for children under 13.
