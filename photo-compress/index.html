Photo Resize Compressor

A simple iPhone app for reducing photo file sizes, resizing images, and saving clean new copies without changing the originals.

Reduce File Size

Adjust image quality to make photos lighter while keeping the original photo unchanged.

Resize Photos

Choose presets for blogs, social posts, shops, and stories, or enter custom pixel dimensions.

Formats and Privacy

Save as JPG, PNG, or HEIC, and remove EXIF and location metadata when needed.

Photo Processing

  • Selected photos are processed on your device.
  • Photos are not uploaded to the app operator's server.
  • Converted images can be saved to Photos or shared from your device.

The app is free to use and may show ads in some areas. Batch processing may request a rewarded ad; if the ad is unavailable, saving continues.
