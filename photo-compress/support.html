Photo Resize Compressor Support

Photo Resize Compressor helps you reduce photo file sizes, resize images, and save converted images as new files on iPhone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app change my original photos?

No. Converted images are saved as new files. Your original photos stay unchanged.

Are my photos uploaded to a server?

No. Image conversion is processed on your device. Photos are not uploaded to the app operator's server.

Why can batch processing show an ad?

The app is free to use. Batch processing may request a rewarded ad. If the ad is unavailable, saving continues.

Can I use the app without allowing tracking?

Yes. You can continue using the image conversion features if you deny or skip tracking permission.
