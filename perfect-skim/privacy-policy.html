Perfect Skim Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-28

Perfect Skim is developed by hapinesspang. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app.

Information We Do Not Collect Directly

Perfect Skim does not require a custom account. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or precise location.

Game Center

If you use Apple Game Center features, Apple may process your Game Center profile, leaderboard scores, achievements, and related account information according to Apple’s own terms and privacy policy. Perfect Skim uses Game Center only for ranking and competition features.

Advertising

Perfect Skim is designed to use Google AdMob for ads, including optional rewarded ads. AdMob may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, diagnostics, interaction data, and other information used for ad delivery, fraud prevention, measurement, and personalization depending on your device settings and consent choices.

Rewarded ads are optional. The app must not ask you to click ads, and rewards are granted for watching eligible rewarded ads, not for clicking ads.

Purchases

Perfect Skim does not use subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Children

Perfect Skim is intended as a general audience skill game. The app does not contain user-generated content, chat, gambling, or real-money rewards.

Data Sharing

Data handled by Apple Game Center is governed by Apple. Data handled by Google AdMob is governed by Google’s advertising services and applicable consent settings. Perfect Skim does not sell personal information directly.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact hapinesspang@gmail.com.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, ad configuration, analytics, or platform requirements change.
