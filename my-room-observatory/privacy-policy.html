My Room Observatory Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-21

My Room Observatory helps you decide whether tonight is worth stepping outside for the Moon, Venus when visible, International Space Station passes, meteor shower windows, and sky conditions. The app also shows practical viewing tips for the strongest target. It does not require an account and does not operate a custom backend server.

Information Used for Core App Features

  • Location: Used to calculate local sky visibility, ISS pass timing, and the Tonight Score. You can use the app with a regional starter city if you do not grant location permission, and you can switch sky calls back to the starter city from Settings after granting location.
  • Weather and sky conditions: When available, Apple WeatherKit may be used to estimate current sky visibility for the selected area.
  • Notifications: If you enable notifications, the app schedules practical reminders for strong viewing windows, such as a bright ISS pass. You can turn off app-managed useful alerts from Settings in the app without disabling iOS notification permission globally. Notification permission also remains under your control in iOS Settings.
  • Widgets: The app stores a small local snapshot of the latest Tonight Score so Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets can display it.

Public Orbital Data

The app may download public International Space Station orbit data from CelesTrak and process pass predictions on device. Your location is not sent to an app-owned server for ISS calculations.

Advertising and Third-Party SDKs

The app uses Google AdMob to support free access. Depending on consent settings, ATT tracking choice, and SDK behavior, AdMob may collect data such as identifiers, IP address, diagnostics, performance data, advertising data, and ad interaction information for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and service improvement. The app includes Google's consent flow where required by the configured launch region and ad settings, and may ask for Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission when personalized ads or ad measurement require it.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No account, password, or profile is required.
  • No custom app server stores your sky checks.
  • No user-generated telescope images, contacts, calendar events, or health data are collected by the app.

Data Retention

The app may cache recent public ISS orbit data and the latest widget snapshot on your device. If cached ISS data is stale, the app limits timing and alert confidence until it can refresh again. iOS or third-party SDKs may retain diagnostics or advertising-related data according to their own policies and your device settings.

Children

The app is a general audience sky-viewing utility and is not directed to children. Do not use personalized advertising settings for child-directed distribution.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, advertising configuration, data sources, or App Store privacy disclosures change.
