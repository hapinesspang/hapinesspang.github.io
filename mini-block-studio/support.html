Mini Block Studio Support

Help for guided mini block builds, local shelves, and custom creations.

Mini Block Studio is an iPhone app for building small 3D block models, remixing starter ideas, saving local progress, and keeping completed builds on a shelf.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account?

No. The app does not require sign-in or an operator account.

Where is my progress saved?

Guided build progress, completed model IDs, badges, and custom builds are saved locally on your device.

Are my custom builds uploaded?

No. Mini Block Studio does not upload custom builds to an app operator server in version 1.0. Sharing uses the iOS share sheet and only happens when you choose to share.

Does the app show ads or ask for tracking?

No. Version 1.0 is free and ad-free. It does not include purchases, subscriptions, or tracking prompts.

Are all models included?

Yes. All 10 guided models included in version 1.0 are available after install.

How do I use the build guide?

Open a model, rotate the 3D view if needed, then follow the current step card. Place 1 adds the next highlighted brick. Finish Step fills the current 6-piece step at once.

Do I just keep pressing Next?

No. Mini Block Studio is built around small actions. Use Place 1 when you want to follow the model brick by brick, tap the highlighted brick in the 3D view when it is easy to see, or use Finish Step when you already understand the current 6-piece shape.

Why does Sleepy Cat start with a face?

Sleepy Cat begins with a 6-piece cat face so the first step is recognizable before the full 42-piece model is complete.

What are Starter Ideas?

Free Studio includes Cat Face, Tiny House, and Flower Pot starter ideas. Tap one to load a small build, then change colors, add bricks, undo, or save it to your shelf.

Can I delete saved studio creations?

Yes. Open the Shelf tab, find the studio creation, and use the delete button on its card. Deleted creations are removed from the local shelf on your device.
